What We Do

Providing exceptional sales and aftermarket technical support for commercial vehicles.

Quality Spare Parts Distribution

We specialize in importing and distributing high-quality spare parts for commercial vehicles, ensuring that our customers have access to reliable and durable components. By providing top-notch spare parts, we contribute to the maintenance and longevity of commercial vehicles, reducing downtime and operational costs.

Aftermarket Technical Support

We offer comprehensive aftermarket technical support to our customers. This includes advisory maintenance services designed to enhance the efficiency of logistics businesses. By optimizing maintenance processes and offering expert advice, we help our clients reduce costs and improve productivity in their operations.

Consulting Services

Our company goes beyond parts distribution to provide consulting services. We work closely with logistics businesses to identify their specific industrial needs and offer tailored solutions. This approach helps our clients streamline their operations, make informed decisions, and operate more economically.

About Us

Automobile After Market Ghana Ltd is a privately-owned limited liability company registered under the laws of the Republic of Ghana. Our primary focus is on providing exceptional sales and aftermarket technical support for commercial vehicles. Over the years, we have established ourselves as one of Ghana's leading independent distributors and importers of high-quality, affordable spare parts for various commercial vehicles, including Trucks, Buses, Trailers, and Heavy-Duty Vehicles. In addition to our parts distribution services, we also offer valuable consulting and advisory maintenance services to logistics businesses. Our goal is to enhance their operational efficiency, reduce costs, and optimize productivity.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the trusted source for maintenance services and top-notch mechanical parts that not only lower costs but also enhance productivity for our valued customers.

Our Mission

At Automobile After Market Ghana Ltd, we are dedicated to identifying the unique needs of various industries and delivering high-quality services and products in a cost-effective manner.

Services

Our services encompass a comprehensive range of products and support solutions, underscoring our dedication to meeting the needs of Ghana's commercial vehicle industry.

Spare Parts Sales

We offer a wide range of spare parts for commercial vehicles, including trucks, buses, trailers, and heavy-duty vehicles. Our inventory includes items such as roller bearings, clutches, timing belts, brake pads, brake shoes, brake liners, filters, and engine oil from renowned worldwide brands.

Aftermarket Technical Support

Our team of experts provides aftermarket technical support to help businesses maintain and repair their commercial vehicles. We offer guidance on maintenance best practices, troubleshooting, and repairs to ensure vehicles operate efficiently and safely.

Consulting and Advisory Services

We provide consulting services to logistics businesses, helping them identify industry-specific needs and challenges. Our advisory services aim to optimize business processes, reduce operating costs, and enhance overall productivity.

Product Sourcing

In addition to our stocked inventory, we offer product sourcing services to help customers find specific spare parts and components that may not be readily available. We leverage our industry connections to locate and procure these items.

Product Distribution

We import and distribute spare parts from worldwide brands, ensuring that our customers have access to a diverse range of high-quality products. Our distribution network ensures timely delivery to our clients.

Customer Support

Our commitment to excellent customer service means we are readily available to address customer inquiries, provide product information, and assist with order processing. Customers can reach out to us via email or phone for prompt assistance.
